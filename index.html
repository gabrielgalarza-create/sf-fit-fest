Programming

Six zones. One unforgettable day.

The festival is built around six programmable zones, each presented by a partner brand. Tap any card for details.

presented by New Balance

Pre-festival Run

9:15 AM · led by Midnight Runners

A 3-mile lead-in run with the Bay's loudest crew before doors open. New Balance silhouettes available to trial along the route.

main programming

Group Workouts

HIIT · Full-body strength · Mat pilates

Three back-to-back coached classes on the main stage with our top instructors. Modifications offered for every move, every level welcome.

presented by Red Bull

Pre-Workout Energy Zone

All day · sampling + DJ booth

Caffeine, hype, and a pre-workout sampling tent to lock in before every block. The Red Bull DJ booth keeps the energy high all day.

presented by Kalos Health

Recovery Zone

All day · IV, massage, chiro

On-site IV hydration, sports massages, and chiropractic adjustments. Designed to keep you moving and feeling better than when you walked in.

presented by Volo Sports

Pickleball Court

12:00 PM · bracket play

Bracket-play tournament hosted by Volo Sports. Sign up solo or with a partner. Volo merch and league credits up for grabs.

presented by Nectar Collective

Social Zone

2:00 PM · DJ + salsa social

Premium sparkling cocktails with natural fruit, botanicals, and real honey. An open salsa social and a live DJ set close out the day.
