Programming

Four zones. One unforgettable day.

The festival is built around four distinct zones plus a Volo pickleball court and a kickoff run with Midnight Runners. Tap any card to see what's inside.

Zone 01

Group Workouts Zone

Coached strength & cardio on the main stage

Two back-to-back coached workouts plus the day's fitness competitions (40-yard dash, shuttle challenge). Step decks, resistance bands, and an outdoor sound system. Open to every level, with modifications offered for every move.

Zone 02

Recovery Zone

IV hydration, massages, chiro adjustments

On-site IV therapy, sports massage, and chiropractic adjustments, plus a stretching and mobility tent. Designed to keep you moving between blocks and walking out feeling better than you walked in.

Zone 03

Workout Prep Zone

Pre & post workout treats, all day

Fuel up before you train and refuel after. Pre-workout snacks and hydration to get you primed; protein, recovery treats, and a flavor sampling bar waiting on the back end. Stocked all day by Spindrift, Red Bull, and partner brands.

Zone 04

Social Zone

DJ set, salsa social, alcoholic seltzers

When you're done sweating, the social zone takes over. A live DJ set powers an open salsa social, alcoholic seltzers from partner brands flow at the bar, and the chillest spot on the field becomes the place to make new friends and link up with the community.

presented by Volo Sports

Pickleball Tournament

12:00 PM, bracket play

A full bracket pickleball tournament hosted by Volo Sports. Sign up solo or with a partner at registration. Volo merch and league credits up for grabs for the winners.

with Midnight Runners

Pre-festival Run

9:15–9:45 AM, 3 miles, open to all

Kick the day off with the Bay's loudest run crew. Midnight Runners leads a 3-mile lead-in run from the venue, returning back as doors open. Bring your shoes; we bring the energy.

with New Balance

New Balance Shoe Trials

All day, try the latest drops

Test-run the latest New Balance training and running silhouettes on-site. Trial sessions on the turf and the track-side run lane, staffed by NB reps with sizing and giveaways.

brand activation lane

Partner Pop-ups

Rivian, Red Bull, Kalos & more

A dedicated activation lane lined with partner pop-ups: Rivian test drives and lead capture, the Red Bull DJ booth, Kalos Health on-site consults, and rotating partner experiences throughout the day.
