Programming

Eleven zones. One unforgettable day.

The festival is built around eleven programmable zones, most presented by a partner brand. Tap any card for details.

presented by New Balance

Pre-festival Run

9:15 AM · led by Midnight Runners

A 3-mile lead-in run with the Bay's loudest crew before doors open. New Balance silhouettes available to trial along the route.

Led by Coach Daniel · The Facility

HIIT Group Workout

10:15 AM · max 40 people

Coach Daniel of The Facility opens the day with a high-intensity interval workout on the main turf. Capped at 40 to keep it intimate, with modifications for every level. The block continues with full-body strength at 11:15 and mat pilates at 12:15.

main programming

Pilates Zone

12:15 – 1:15 PM · max 40 people

Mat pilates on the main turf, coached and recovery-focused. Capped at 40 with modifications for every level. Brings down the intensity from the morning workout block while keeping the body moving.

presented by Volo Sports

Pickleball Court

11 AM – 2 PM · bracket play

Three hours of bracket-play tournament hosted by Volo Sports. Sign up solo or with a partner. Volo merch and league credits up for grabs.

presented by Nectar Collective

Social Zone

12 – 3 PM · DJ, food, Nectar cocktails

Ready-to-drink cocktails to unwind from your workout and enjoy the festival atmosphere.

production by DJ Jaymee Baaby

Live DJ Entertainment Zone

All day · DJ Jaymee Baaby on the decks

The festival's sonic backbone. DJ Jaymee Baaby anchors the day from open to close, building from morning workout energy to a 3 PM headliner peak.

presented by Red Bull

Pre-Workout Energy Zone

All day · sampling + DJ booth

Caffeine, hype, and a pre-workout sampling tent to lock in before every block. The Red Bull DJ booth keeps the energy high all day.

presented by Kalos Health

Recovery Zone

All day · IV, massage, chiro

On-site IV hydration, sports massages, and chiropractic adjustments. Designed to keep you moving and feeling better than when you walked in.

Led by Inessence Studios

Salsa Dance Class Zone

12 – 1 PM · open floor

Open-floor salsa class on the dance pad. All levels welcome, no partner required.

Led by Mbere Moves

Afrocardio Zone

1 – 2 PM · open floor

High-energy afrocardio class on the dance pad. All levels welcome, follow along.

main programming

World Cup Watch Party

All day · live screen

Live World Cup matches on-screen all day. Cheer your team between blocks with food, drinks, and the festival crew.

Operational asks

What we need from the venue.

A short list of the specific things we need locked in to deliver the festival.

  • Liquor license access

    For Nectar Collective beverage service at the bar.

  • Power + audio for the DJ setup

    Across the venue, wherever the DJs need to plug in.

  • Screen placements for World Cup viewing

    Confirm where the venue's two jumbo screens will live in the space.

  • Three hours of pickleball court access

    Ideally all five courts. If not, let us know what makes the most sense and we'll scale down. Open to the public.

  • Best space for the Recovery Zone

    For massage tables and recovery drinks. Looking for a calm pocket of the venue.

  • Best locations for check-ins

    We've marked our suggested entry points on the site map. Confirm what works for the venue.
