The vision

A full day of training, recovery, and connection — built for SF's most ambitious community.

SF Fit Fest is OTT's flagship outdoor fitness festival. We bring together hundreds of high-earning, wellness-minded young professionals for group workouts, pickleball, salsa, fitness competitions, recovery experiences, and standout brand activations — capped with a pool party social that turns the day into a vibe.

Programming

Tap any card to see what's inside.

with Midnight Runners

Pre-festival run

9:15–9:45 AM · 3 miles · open to all

Kick the day off with the Bay's loudest run crew. Midnight Runners leads a 3-mile lead-in run from the venue, returning back as doors open. Bring your shoes; we bring the energy.

Main stage

Group workout sessions

10:30 AM · strength + cardio

Two back-to-back coached workouts on the main stage. Step decks, resistance bands, and an outdoor sound system. Open to every level — modifications offered for every move.

Live instruction

Salsa class

11:30 AM · all levels

Bay Area salsa instructors take the stage for a 30-minute class designed to get the whole festival moving — beginners welcome, no partner needed.

presented by Volo Sports

Pickleball tournament

12:00 PM · bracket play

A full bracket pickleball tournament hosted by Volo Sports — sign up solo or with a partner at registration. Volo merch and league credits up for grabs for the winners.

Branded prizes

Fitness competitions

12:30 PM · 40-yard dash + shuttle challenge

Two open competitions — the 40-yard dash and a shuttle agility challenge. Sign up day-of, fastest times take home branded prize packs from our partners.

with Kalos Health

Recovery + wellness lounge

All day · IV, stretching, consults

A full recovery zone with hydration IV, mobility coaches, on-the-spot consultations from Kalos Health, and a stretching tent — designed to keep you moving between blocks and into the afterparty.

with New Balance

New Balance shoe trials

All day · try the latest drops

Test-run the latest New Balance training and running silhouettes on-site. Trial sessions on the turf and the track-side run lane — staffed by NB reps with sizing and giveaways.

powered by Red Bull

Red Bull DJ booth

All day · live sets between blocks

Red Bull's branded DJ booth headlines the soundscape between programming blocks. Cans iced and ready, parasols up, DJs spinning until the day rolls into the pool party.

presenting sponsor: Spindrift

Post-workout refresh zone

All day · flavor tasting bar

A fully branded post-workout refresh zone with the full Spindrift lineup — sampling station, flavor bar, and a chill-out area to refuel between sessions.

Summer Edition exclusive

Pool party afterparty

After 3 PM · venue revealed to ticket holders

Summer Edition only — the festival flows into a private pool party social. DJs, drinks from our partners, and the community we built across the day. Limited capacity.
