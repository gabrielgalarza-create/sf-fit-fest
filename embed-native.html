The vision

Workouts, recovery, festival DJs, and World Cup watch party.

SF Fit Fest is OTT's flagship outdoor fitness festival. Hundreds of high-earning, wellness-minded young professionals, all in one place at The Crossing at East Cut.

Programming

Eleven zones. One unforgettable day.

Led by Midnight Runners

Pre-festival Run

9:15 – 10 AM

A 3-mile lead-in run with the Bay's loudest crew before doors open. New Balance silhouettes available to trial along the route.

Led by Coach Daniel · The Facility

HIIT Group Workout

10:15 – 11:15 AM

Coach Daniel of The Facility opens the day with a high-intensity interval workout on the main turf, with modifications for every level. Up to 50 spots to start. The block flows into mat pilates at 11:15 in the same space.

Led by Coach Starr · FitStarr

Pilates Zone

11:15 AM – 12:15 PM

Coach Starr of FitStarr leads mat pilates on the main turf — coached, recovery-focused, with modifications for every level. Up to 50 spots to start. Brings down the intensity from the morning HIIT block while keeping the body moving.

Led by Coach Leah · Strong Babes

HIIT & Bands

12:15 – 1:15 PM

Coach Leah of Strong Babes leads a high-intensity HIIT + resistance bands session — strength meets conditioning, with modifications for every level.

presented by Volo Sports & 510 Society

Pickleball Court

10 AM – 2 PM · bracket play

Three hours of bracket-play tournament hosted by Volo Sports and 510 Society. Register through Volo's site to claim your spot — solo or with a partner. Merch and league credits up for grabs.

presented by Nectar Collective

Social Zone

12 – 4 PM · Nectar cocktails & food vendors

Ready-to-drink cocktails from Nectar Collective and accessible food vendors on site.

production by DJ Jaymee Baaby

Live DJ Entertainment

9:45 AM – 1:45 PM · DJ Jaymee Baaby on the decks

The festival's sonic backbone. DJ Jaymee Baaby spins from 9:45 AM to 1:45 PM, scoring the workout blocks from the morning warm-up through the midday peak.

production by DJ Umami

DJ Set with DJ Umami

11:45 AM – 12:45 PM

DJ Umami takes the decks for the mid-day stretch — a high-energy hour scoring lunch and the transition into the afternoon block.

presented by Red Bull

Pre-Workout Energy Zone

All day · sampling + DJ booth

Caffeine, hype, and a pre-workout sampling tent to lock in before every block. The Red Bull DJ booth keeps the energy high all day.

presented by Bay Fit Collective

Recovery Zone

All day · IV, massage, chiro

On-site IV hydration, sports massages, and chiropractic adjustments by Bay Fit Collective. Designed to keep you moving and feeling better than when you walked in.

Led by Inessence Studios

Salsa Dance Class Zone

12 – 1 PM · open floor

Open-floor salsa class on the dance pad. All levels welcome, no partner required.

Led by Mbere Moves

Afrocardio Zone

1 – 2 PM · open floor

High-energy afrocardio class on the dance pad. All levels welcome, follow along.

Hosted by The East Cut

Soccer Game Viewing

All day · live screen

Live soccer matches on-screen all day. Cheer your team between blocks with food, drinks, and the festival crew.

Run of show

A day designed to flow.

Two phases: fitness programming runs from 9:15 AM – 2 PM, then the festival energy takes over from 2 – 4 PM with the live DJ sets, drinks, and World Cup viewing.

Venue & site map

The Crossing at East Cut.

250 Main Street, San Francisco. 92,000 SF activated across eleven programmable zones, mapped to the venue's existing infrastructure: 5-court pickleball complex, outdoor cinema with built-in 8'×20' screen, two sound systems, and a full-service bar.
